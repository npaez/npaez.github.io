About me

My name is Nestor Paez, and I am a Computer Science student based in Argentina. Nowadays, I'm a developer at Sinaptica Labs. Currently, we are developing an open source e-commerce with base in Node.JS, Express and MongoDB.

I always like computer systems and programming, but it was not until a few years after finishing high school, I realized that this is something I am passionate about doing. Since then, I've been turning coffee into code and am very happy doing this labor.
